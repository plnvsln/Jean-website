If you wish to proceed with a commission please contact me, we will establish your requirements, including APPROX. size, price & delivery date.

Once you are ready to proceed please send high resolution images of your chosen subject via email/whatsApp & provide the actual size you require. In turn I will confirm which of the images I have chosen to use, & provide you with an ACTUAL price & completion date. ​

Upon completion of the artwork I will send you an image via email/whatsApp to check that you are completely happy with it; once approved I will request payment in full and dispatch your artwork upon receipt of your funds.
