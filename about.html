About Me

I was born within the sound of Bow Bells, East London and at the age of five I moved with my family to Hampshire which is where I still live.

Throughout my childhood, my favourite pastime was drawing and I was encouraged by those who saw my work telling me I had a natural talent.

On leaving school I worked for a large organisation. My colleagues soon became aware of my love for art & asked me to draw/paint pictures for them. Word of my artwork travelled fast & and I shortly had an endless stream of paying customers with commissions mainly for portraits of customers pets, family & friends.

Art commissions gave way to nurturing years of motherhood. Upon my children becoming self-sufficient & leaving home I was very happy to once again, resume my commissioned based work.

I can honestly to say that I thoroughly love & enjoy my work as an artist. I have great pleasure in completing commissions to extremely happy & satisfied customers!! ​
